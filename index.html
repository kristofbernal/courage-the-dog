Courage the Cowardly Dog

About Courage

Personality

Courage, due to his traumatic past, is often an anxious and shy dog. However, when his family -- especially his mother, Muriel -- is in danger, he gets all giddy and with all his strength, saves them from the mysterious dangers that arrive in Nowhere, Kansas.

Muriel, Courage, and Eustace.

History

Long story short: A clinic visit turns horrifying when a doctor made Courage's parents disappear. Courage flees and ends up next to a garbage dump, where she is seen by a lovely little lady named Muriel. After convincing her husband, Muriel takes Courage home and becomes their child, and then they live happily ever after. Not.

Learn more about Courage's history here.

Powers

  1. Courage has amazing lungs. He can scream as loud as possible. Reportedly, he also has a better chance of beating COVID-19.
  2. He can shapeshift. However, this is merely for show.
  3. He has great pain tolerance. He can probably survive a nuclear attack.
  4. Additionally, he can heal from injuries faster than you can say "hospital".
  5. He has amazing pocket space, since he often pulls things from behind him or from thin air.
  6. His other humanly skills include: cooking, coding, crafting, and much more.
